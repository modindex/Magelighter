-------------------------------------------
Magelighter
-------------------------------------------

A Mod for Minecraft, Magelighter hopes to bring Magic and Adventure to your worlds. It's inspired by Brandon Sanderson's Cosmere, Dungeon's & Dragons, and the Witcher Mod from 1.7.
